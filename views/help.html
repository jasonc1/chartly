Create charts for a proof of concept - without collecting data
Whether you're trying to make a data visualization prototype and you don't want to collect/make up user data and design your own chart or just need a chart quick and easy. Chartly can provide you a customizable graphic.

Chartly has four basic options (more coming).

Bar Chart - for displaying data side by side as bars
Line Chart - for plotting data points on a line
Radar Chart - for showing mutliple data points & variation.
Pie Chart - for showing proportions of data
